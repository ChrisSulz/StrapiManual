02 Expert Lectures

A Create a new expert lecture

Visit the page https://test.skb-virtuell.de:8080/admin/

Click on Content Manager

Click on Fachvortrag

You are now in section Fachvortrag

Click on Create new entry

Input a aussteller entry

Input a datum entry

Choose a start_time entry

Choose a end_time entry

Input a title entry

Write something into the text field

Click on Save

Click on Publish

B Delete an expert lecture

Visit the page https://test.skb-virtuell.de:8080/admin/

Click on Content Manager

Click on Fachvortrag

You are now in section Fachvortrag

Click on Delete

Click on Confirm
